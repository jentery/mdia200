Ownership Effects

Global media are produced by a few
companies (monopolies and oligopolies),
which advertise everywhere.

Content is driven by mass appeal.

Creativity matters less than profit.

See Kantar's "2023 Global Top 100 infographic," for instance.
Also consider mergers such as Disney + ABC (1995), Google + YouTube (2006),
Comcast + NBC Universal (2011), and Disney + 21st Century Fox (2019).

Culture and Content Industries

Culture industry: popular culture is a factory
for standardized goods that manipulate
society into passivity.

Content industry: "an industry that generates
revenue from the production and/or
circulation of content alone" (Eichhorn)

Content capital: "one's ability to produce
content not about one's work but about
one's status as an artist, writer, or performer" (Eichhorn)
